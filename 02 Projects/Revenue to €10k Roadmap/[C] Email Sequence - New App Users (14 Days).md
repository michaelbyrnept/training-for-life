# Email Sequence: New App Users
**Segment:** Anyone who signs up to the app with marketing consent
**Platform:** Kit (ConvertKit)
**Trigger:** User added to Kit via form `9528217` on registration
**Goal:** Get them into the 3-Day Programme and book a consultation by Day 14

---

## Kit Setup Notes

1. In Kit, go to **Sequences** and create a new sequence called "New App User Onboarding"
2. Set the trigger: subscriber added to form `9528217`
3. Add each email below as a step with the send delay listed
4. Set sender name to **Michael** and reply-to as `michael@trainingforlife.ie`
5. All emails should come from a personal address, not a brand address. People reply to people.

## Email 5 — Day 10

**Subject:** The training principle most people ignore
**Preview text:** Consistency beats intensity. Every time.

---

Hey [First Name],

Something I see constantly: people who train really hard for 3 weeks, burn out, stop for 2 weeks, go again hard, repeat forever.

They think the problem is motivation. It's not.

The problem is they're treating training like a punishment that needs to be survived rather than a habit that needs to be built.

Here's what the research actually shows: 3 sessions per week, consistently, over 12 months beats 5 sessions per week with regular breaks. Not by a little. By a lot.

Because the compound effect of consistent training is enormous. Your nervous system adapts. Your joints get used to load. Your confidence builds. Your habits get automatic.

The goal of the first 30 days isn't transformation. It's just to make showing up feel normal.

The 3-Day Programme is three sessions. That's it. A perfect first week of that habit.

## What to Track in Kit

- **Open rate target:** 40%+ (personal emails from a real person should hit this)
- **Click rate target:** 8%+ on emails with a CTA
- **Conversion target:** 1 consultation booked per 20 new subscribers

If open rates drop below 30%, the subject lines need work.
If click rates are good but no consultations are booking, the consultation page needs work.

## After This Sequence Ends
